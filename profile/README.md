## Welcome

## What is DeckConnect?
DeckConnect is an native integration that bridges the Elgato Stream Deck to your Unreal application (Editor, Game, App, etc). Literally hotkey _anything_ that you do in Unreal directly to your Stream Deck.

You can share your button profiles with your coworkers and friends, ship Unreal applications with native support for the Stream Deck, add in extra support for streamers to hotkey muting/unmuting mics, and so much more!

It's perfect for GameDev and demoing at conventions!
